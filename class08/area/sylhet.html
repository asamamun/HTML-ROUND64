About Sylhet Division

Sylhet Division, located in the northeastern part of Bangladesh, is renowned for its stunning tea gardens, lush hills, and cultural diversity. It's a region of natural beauty and vibrant traditions.

Top Attractions

  • Sreemangal Tea Gardens
  • Jaflong and Khasi Hills
  • Ratnodweep (Ratnodweep Island)
  • Ratargul Swamp Forest
  • Sylhet Shahjalal University of Science and Technology
