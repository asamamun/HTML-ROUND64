About Khulna Division

Khulna Division, located in the southwestern part of Bangladesh, is famous for its unique natural beauty, the Sundarbans mangrove forest, and vibrant culture. It's a hub of trade and tourism.

Top Attractions

  • Sundarbans Mangrove Forest
  • Shat Gambuj Mosque (Sixty Dome Mosque)
  • Bagerhat Historic Mosque City
  • Khulna Shipyard Beach
  • Khulna Royal Palace
