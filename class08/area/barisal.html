About Barisal Division

Barisal Division, located in the southern part of Bangladesh, is a land of picturesque rivers and lush greenery. With a rich cultural heritage, it offers a unique blend of natural beauty and historical significance.

Top Attractions

  • Kuakata Sea Beach
  • Haringhata River Boat Ride
  • Durga Sagar Temple
  • Guthia Mosque
  • Lebur Char
