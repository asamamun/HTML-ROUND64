About Mymensingh Division

Mymensingh Division, situated in the central-northern part of Bangladesh, is a region known for its scenic beauty, educational institutions, and rich agricultural heritage. It's a land of diversity and culture.

Top Attractions

  • Brahmaputra River
  • Shashi Lodge
  • Shambhuganj Bridge
  • Muktagacha Rajbari
  • Birishiri Stone Quarry
