About Chittagong Division

Chittagong Division, located in the southeastern part of Bangladesh, is a region renowned for its natural beauty, coastal areas, and diverse culture. It's a perfect blend of tradition and modernity.

Top Attractions

  • Cox's Bazar Beach
  • Sundarbans Mangrove Forest
  • Foy's Lake
  • Patenga Sea Beach
  • Chittagong Hill Tracts
