About Rajshahi Division

Rajshahi Division, located in the northwestern part of Bangladesh, is famous for its silk industry, archaeological sites, and historical significance. It's a region rich in culture and history.

Top Attractions

  • Paharpur Vihara
  • Mahasthangarh
  • Varendra Research Museum
  • Sompur Bihara
  • Puthia Temple Complex
