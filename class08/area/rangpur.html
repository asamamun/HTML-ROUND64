About Rangpur Division

Rangpur Division, located in the northwestern part of Bangladesh, is known for its agriculture, serene landscapes, and warm hospitality. It's a region where rural charm meets modern development.

Top Attractions

  • Kantojiu Temple
  • Tajhat Palace
  • Mithapukur Zamindar Bari
  • Payra Sea Port
  • Teesta Barrage
