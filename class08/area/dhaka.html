About Dhaka Division

Dhaka Division, the capital region of Bangladesh, is a vibrant and dynamic area known for its historical landmarks, modern infrastructure, and cultural diversity.

Top Attractions

  • Sonargaon Ancient City
  • Jatiya Sangsad Bhaban (National Parliament House)
  • Ahsan Manzil (Pink Palace)
  • Lalbagh Fort
  • Dhakeshwari Temple
